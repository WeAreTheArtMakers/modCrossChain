Cross-chain execution with one clear decision surface.

modCrossChain is a non-custodial bridge interface for Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, Base, Arbitrum, and Avalanche. It requests the best route from LI.FI, keeps signing in the wallet, and shows costs before the user confirms.

modCrossChain; Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, Base, Arbitrum ve Avalanche icin gelistirilmis, saklamasiz bir bridge arayuzudur. En uygun rotayi LI.FI uzerinden alir, imzayi cuzdanda tutar ve kullanici onaylamadan once maliyeti gosterir.

Experience

Deneyim

The live app is intentionally stripped back to the bridge.

The Railway application no longer carries a marketing panel. It opens with one job: choose the route, review the fee, sign in the wallet, and track the transfer.

Railway uzerindeki uygulama artik pazarlama paneli tasimiyor. Tek amaci; rotayi secmek, ucreti gormek, cuzdanda imzalamak ve transferi takip etmek.

Flow

Akis

Aggregator-first execution keeps the product simple.

The product does not attempt to invent bridge infrastructure. It delegates route search and execution to LI.FI and keeps the frontend responsible for clarity and wallet flow.

Urun kendi bridge protokolunu yazmaya calismaz. Rota aramasi ve yurutmeyi LI.FI'a birakir; arayuz ise netlik ve cuzdan akisina odaklanir.

01

Connect and detect

Connect a wallet, detect the active chain, and set the source environment.

Cuzdani bagla, aktif agi algila ve kaynak ortamini belirle.

02

Quote and compare

Request cheapest, fastest, and best received candidates from a single quote surface.

Tek quote yuzeyinden en ucuz, en hizli ve en iyi net alis seceneklerini getir.

03

Sign and track

Execute through the wallet, then follow status updates, hash visibility, and explorer links.

Islemi cuzdanda yurut, sonra durum, hash ve explorer baglantilarini takip et.

Revenue

Gelir modeli

Per-transfer fee collection with visible disclosure

The clean model is an integrator fee on each bridge. The product should keep the fee visible, avoid hidden markups, and preserve wallet-only execution.

En temiz model, her bridge isleminde integrator fee almaktir. Ucret gorunur kalmali, gizli marj olmamali ve cuzdan odakli akis korunmalidir.

  • Current starter fee: 0.15%
  • Wallet-only execution remains unchanged
  • Fee disclosure is built into the route panel

Security

Guvenlik

Non-custodial by product definition

No backend signer, no stored private key, and no custom bridge protocol to maintain. The frontend validates inputs and leaves execution to the connected wallet.

Arka tarafta imzalayici yok, private key saklanmiyor ve bakimi zor bir ozel bridge protokolu yazilmiyor. Arayuz girdileri dogrular ve islemi bagli cuzdana birakir.

  • Private keys never leave the wallet
  • Token validation runs before quote requests
  • Jurisdiction and terms pages are linked from the app

Stack

Teknik yigin

Lean frontend, aggregator-first backend boundaries.

The application is intentionally narrow: Next.js for product structure, wagmi plus viem for wallet behavior, and LI.FI for route intelligence and execution.

Uygulama bilincli olarak dar tutuldu: urun yapisi icin Next.js, cuzdan davranislari icin wagmi ve viem, rota zekasi ve execution icin LI.FI kullaniliyor.
